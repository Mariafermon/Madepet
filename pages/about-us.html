    Mary and Lisa

    Maria and Ana. They have been best friends since childhood and have always shared a love for animals. When they graduated from college, they decided to move in together and adopt a cute little dog named Max.
    As pet owners, they faced a common problem - finding reliable and trustworthy professionals for Max's care when they were busy with work or out of town. They realized that other pet owners might be facing the same issue and wanted to do something to help. That's when they came up with the idea for Madepet - a platform that would connect pet owners with the best professionals for their pets' needs. With their shared passion for animals and their complementary skills in business and technology, Maria and Ana were the perfect team to bring this idea to life.
    Today, Madepet has grown into a thriving community of pet owners and professionals who share a love for animals and a commitment to their well-being. Maria and Ana are proud of what they have achieved and are constantly working to improve the platform and make pet care easier and more accessible for everyone.

    What is MADEPET?

    MADEPET is a comprehensive digital platform that connects pet owners with top-notch professionals in the pet care industry. From veterinarians to dog walkers, groomers, and more, MADEPET offers a convenient and reliable solution for finding the perfect professional for your beloved companion. Our mission is to provide a seamless and personalized experience for both pet owners and professionals. With MADEPET, you can easily search and connect with qualified experts who are passionate about delivering exceptional care to your pets. Whether you need routine veterinary services, grooming sessions, or reliable pet sitting, MADEPET has you covered. Our platform allows you to browse through profiles, read reviews, and choose the perfect professional who aligns with your pet's unique needs and your personal preferences. We understand that your pet is a cherished member of your family, and that's why we prioritize safety, trust, and excellence in all aspects of our platform. Join the MADEPET community today and experience the convenience, peace of mind, and exceptional care that your furry friend deserves.
